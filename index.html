Melts in your mouth

The web is a responsive place, from your lithe & lively development process to your end user's super-tablet-multi-magic-laptop-phone. You need grids that are powerful, but custom. Reliable, but responsive.

Susy grids are fluid on the inside, ready to respond at any moment, but contained in the candy shell of your choice, so they respond how and when and where you want them to.

Everywhere you want to be

Susy is based on Natalie Downe's CSS Systems, made possible by Sass, and made easy with Compass. This site was built on HTML5 Boilerplate with Solarized colors, a Modular Scale, and Font Awesome icons.

Susy plays well with everyone, from static sites to python/django, ruby/rails, and php/wordpress. From SMACSS to OOCS, Bootstrap, HTML5 Boilerplate, and 320 and up. It even comes packaged with Middleman, to make your life easy.
